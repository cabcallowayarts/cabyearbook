The Cab Calloway School of the Arts Yearbook is no ordinary book. The Cab Calloway School of the Arts yeabook and photography staff work hard every year to produce a high quality, student-designed yearbook that encompasses all the amazing things that go on in and around the school. Our photographers are on the scene of all major events and happenings around the school to make sure we document your favorite moments of the year.
